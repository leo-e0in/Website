Building my Own PC

Early 2025 i got into PC hardware, therefore wanting to make my own. For this i had to set a budget and research components. I ended up going with the following:

  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 7 5700g (16) @ 4.6 Mhz
  • GPU: AMD ATI Radeon RX 580 ARMOR 8GB OC
  • RAM: Corsair Vengeance LPX 32GB (2 x 16GB) DDR4 (3200 Mhz)
  • Storage: Samsung 970 EVO Plus 1TB NVMe SSD
  • Motherboard: ASUS TUF GAMING A520M-PLUS WIFI
  • Power Supply: MSI MAG A550BN
  • Case: Cooler Master Q300L V2
